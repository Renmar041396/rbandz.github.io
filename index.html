- HOW I SEE TECHNOLOGY

Technology is not just a tool for learning, but it is also an integral part of the learning process. Technology can be used in Academe to help students learn more efficiently and effectively. It can also be used as a tool for training in order to improve the quality of training programs. It's important to think critically about technology and its impacts on society. We have to ask ourselves: How does technology affect people's lives? What are the implications of this new digital world?
